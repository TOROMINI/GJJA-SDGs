Goals . 03

No Poverty
빈곤 퇴치

Why we need this?

The increase in poverty is a very serious problem in that it has a negative impact on government

finances, such as a decrease in the taxpayer population and an increase in social security costs.

solutions

It is necessary to have the will to get out of poverty through vocational training and education,

and it can be solved through national efforts to provide subsidies

such as basic living expenses, medical expenses,

and education expenses to the poor and create jobs.

    Effects

    By eradicating poverty, the gap between the rich and the poor can be reduced,

    resulting in social stability.
