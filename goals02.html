Goals . 02

Quality Education
양질의 교육

Why we need this?

The global educational gap is widening.

For example, in India, many children have to engage in housework or paid labor, so the primary school enrollment rate is close to 100%, but it is known that less than 50% complete all 8 years of primary education.

To solve these problems, quality education is urgently needed.

solutions

Building more schools to make education more accessible to students and providing financial benefits

in the form of scholarships to students who attend classes are also good solutions.

    Effects

    By providing more people with an effective educational environment, they will be able to aim for

    higher educational attainment, which will provide an opportunity to meet other goals of sustainable

     development, such as poverty eradication.
